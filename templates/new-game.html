Business Considerations:

  • Each cup of lemonade requires four ingredients.
  • You can only restock supplies for the upcoming day. Plan ahead.
  • You must maintain a balance above $0 to stay in business.
  • People buy more lemonade on hot and sunny days. Watch the weather forecast.
  • The cheaper you set the cost per cup (compared to competitor stands), the more customers you will earn.
  • There is a $3 property tax at the end of each business day for using your parents' lawn.
  • All unused ice melts at the end of each day.
